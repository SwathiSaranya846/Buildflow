FAQ

Common questions

  • Can I migrate from Jira or Asana?
    Yes — we have a one-click importer for both. Your tasks, assignees, and comments transfer over. The whole process takes under 10 minutes for most teams.
  • Is there a self-hosted option?
    Not yet, though it's on our roadmap. Currently BuildFlow is cloud-only with SOC 2 Type II compliance and data residency options for EU customers.
  • What happens when my free trial ends?
    Your account automatically moves to the Starter (free) plan. You won't lose any data — projects over the limit are archived and restored the moment you upgrade.
  • Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?
    Yes — 50% off Pro for verified nonprofits, and free Pro for students with a valid .edu email. Reach out to billing@buildflow.app with proof of eligibility.
  • How does the Deadline Radar actually work?
    Deadline Radar analyzes task dependencies, historical team velocity, and assignee workload. If it predicts a task will slip, it flags it 48 hours ahead and suggests actions — reassigning, breaking the task up, or adjusting the deadline.
